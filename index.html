Adventures in Costume.

Who Am I?

Hi! I’m Lara. I’m a science student who by night and weekends loves to make costumes and dress up as my favourite characters. I learned to sew my own clothes in primary school and never lost my love of halloween and dress up days, so discovering cosplay was an epiphany for me. At first I was very shy and self conscious, but I persevered and am proud to have learned many new skills, made heaps of awesome friends and had amazing opportunities. I can’t wait to see where cosplay takes me next!

Commissions

Many costumes require extra special details to bring them to life. I've worked with leather, resin, lights and silicone - whatever it takes to make something that's out of this world!

Cosplay

I frequent pop-culture and comic conventions in Melbourne and I hope to see you at one soon! These are a great opportunity to bring out an A-game costume or remake the entire cast of a series with friends.

Photoshoots

There are many friendly and talented photographers in Melbourne who are prepared to scale cliffs, descend waterfalls and get utterly lost in the mountains to capture the perfect photo. I am always ready to plan the next adventure with anyone who’d like to join me.

Events

One of the best parts of cosplay is seeing the reaction people coming face to face with their favourite character, especially kids! I have attended close to 50 charity events already, please get in touch if you think your next event could use more superheroes!
